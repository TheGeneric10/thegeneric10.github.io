About TheGeneric

EST. 2025 · THEGENERIC10 · V26.1

Who We Are

TheGeneric is an independent development organization focused on Minecraft mods, web-based games, and community-driven tools. We believe great software should be accessible, free, and well-crafted.

What We Build

Mo'CharmsMod — A Minecraft Java Edition mod adding powerful charm items with strategic potion effects for survival gameplay. Available on Modrinth for versions 1.6 through 1.20+.

SLOPE — A fast-paced HTML5 arcade runner playable in any browser on any device. No install. No cost.

Our Philosophy

No pay-to-win. No bloat. Every release is tested, balanced, and maintained. If we ship it, we stand behind it.
