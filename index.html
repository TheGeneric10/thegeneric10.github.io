About TheGeneric

EST. 2024 · THEGENERIC10 · V26.1

Who We Are

TheGeneric is an independent development organization focused on Minecraft mods, web-based games, and community-driven tools. We believe great software should be accessible, free, and built with genuine craft.

What We Build

Mo'CharmsMod — A Minecraft mod adding powerful charm items with strategic potion effects for survival gameplay. Available on Modrinth for NeoForge 1.21.8+.

SLOPE — A fast-paced HTML5 arcade runner playable in any browser on any device. No install. No cost. No compromise.

TheGeneric Platform — A unified hub for accounts, social profiles, leaderboards, mod discovery, and community tools — all connected through one identity.

Our Philosophy

No pay-to-win. No bloat. No filler. Every release is tested, balanced, and maintained. If we ship it, we stand behind it — fully and permanently.

History

TheGeneric began in October 2024 as a personal project to build something worth using. From a single concept and early branding experiments, it has grown into a multi-product platform with Modrinth presence, a live game, and a community identity built from the ground up.
